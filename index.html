Portfolio

Hover through some of the projects I have worked on.

Sign Language Translator

A computer visison model based on OpenCV and Tesnorflow's Object detection model that can classify a series of hand sings and detect what the corresponding label. This model works on a live video feed and on images, boxing in the detected object.

Live Car Object Detection

A computer visison model based on OpenCV and Tesnorflow's Object detection model that can classify a series of vehicles. and detect what the corresponding label. This model works on a live video feed and on images, boxing in the detected object.

macOS Motion Controller

With a combination of mediapipe and osascript I was able to perform certain function on my MacBook through body movement. By calculating the angle between nodes on my body I was able to assign play, pause, volume control, and app launch functions.

Dog Breed Image Convolution

A combination of Google Colab, Tensorflow, MatplotLib, Pandas, NumPy and a Kaggle provided dataset allowed me to create a predictive model that helps predict whether the breed of a dog from just the image.

Heart Disease Classification

A combination of Jupyter Notebook, Scikit-Learn, MatplotLib, Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn and a Kaggle provided dataset allowed me to create a classification model that helps predict whether a patient has heart disease given certain clinical parameters.

California Housing Regression

A combination of Jupyter Notebook, Scikit-Learn, MatplotLib, Pandas, NumPy, Seaborn and a Kaggle provided dataset allowed me to create a regression model that helps predict the price of a home in California based on it's location and some other factors.

PC LED Lights

Used an Arduino Uno with potentiometer's, switch's, and transistors to control LED light strips within the PC, displaying both static and dynamic color ranges. The system was powered through the motherboard and the switches were physically screwed into the back plate.

Basketball Mini Hoop Shot Tracker

Used an Arduino Uno with a HC-SR04 (Ultrasonic distance sensor) and the MPU-6050 (accelerometer and gyro) to track a basketball shot, and whether it's a make or miss which would then be displayed on a LCD screen.
